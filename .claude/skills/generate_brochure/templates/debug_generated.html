WSQ - Information Security Management & Compliance Frameworks

About This Course

The Information Security Management & Compliance Frameworks course provides learners with a comprehensive understanding of how to establish, implement, and maintain an effective Information Security Management System (ISMS). Participants will learn how to conduct operational security reviews to identify and mitigate network and information risks, develop robust security controls and parameters, and apply data-driven strategies to strengthen organizational security safeguards. The course also covers the design and implementation of incident response and recovery plans, ensuring multi-layered defense mechanisms capable of addressing security breaches and system vulnerabilities.

Effective information security management is critical for organizations seeking to protect sensitive data and maintain resilient IT infrastructures in an increasingly complex threat environment. This course is especially relevant for IT professionals, security managers, and compliance or risk personnel who are responsible for managing information security programs, mitigating cyber risks, and ensuring organizational resilience. Learners will gain the skills needed to enhance security posture, improve risk management practices, and safeguard information assets against evolving threats.

What You'll Learn

By end of course, learners should be able to:

  • Lead operational reviews of information security processes to identify network risks and enhance security safeguards.
  • Formulate network security parameters using data-driven strategies and system intelligence insights.
  • Drive recovery plans that strengthen multi-layered defence mechanisms.

Frequently Asked Questions

* What are the prerequisites for WSQ Funding?

  1. You need to be a Singaporean Citizen or Permanent Resident, physically based in Singapore.
  2. You must successfully complete the programme and pass the assessment in order to be eligible.
  3. You must attend at least 75% of the training.

* Can I club any other grant with this subsidy?

No, you cannot claim any other grant if you are claiming this subsidy from SSG. You should not be claiming for any other grants, subsidies, or tax concessions, provided unless explicitly permitted.

* Do I need to pay the full fee, and then claim the subsidy from WSQ Funding?

The programme works on a Nett fee model, i.e. you only need to pay the difference between the fee, and the funding amount at the time of enrollment. The training provider (TP) will claim the funding amount from SSG on completion of the programme. In case you fail to complete the programme, or if the claim raised by TP is rejected by SSG then you are liable to pay the funding amount to TP.
